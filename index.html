Step 1: Press any key on the keyboard.

During this step the game will automatically play the first sound and flash the corresponding button.

Step 2: Click the same color that just went off.

During this step if guessed correctly, another sound will play.

Step 3: Click the original colors in sequence and click the additional color that was added.

Each time you answer the sequence correctly, you will level up.

Step 4: Have fun and try to beat your highscore!
